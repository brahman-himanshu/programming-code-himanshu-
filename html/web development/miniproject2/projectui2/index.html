Health Protection Tips

here's how to protect yourself:

Wear a Mask

Use N95 or N99 masks when going outside. Regular cloth masks offer limited protection against fine particles.

Use Air Purifiers

Keep windows closed and use air purifiers with HEPA filters indoors, especially in bedrooms.

Limit Outdoor Activities

Avoid strenuous exercise outdoors. Schedule outdoor activities for times when pollution levels are lower.

Keep Car Windows Up

When driving, keep windows closed and use air recirculation mode in your vehicle.

Add Indoor Plants

Certain plants like spider plants, peace lilies, and snake plants can help improve indoor air quality.

Use Humidifiers

Maintain optimal humidity levels (40-60%) to help reduce particulate matter in the air.
