Prevention Tips

Practical steps you can take to protect yourself and reduce pollution

Wear a Mask

Use N95 or N99 masks when AQI is above 200 to filter out harmful particles.

Use Air Purifiers

Keep indoor air clean with HEPA air purifiers, especially during high pollution days.

Limit Vehicle Use

Combine errands, use public transport, or carpool to reduce vehicular emissions.

Add Indoor Plants

Plants like snake plant and peace lily can help improve indoor air quality.

Avoid Outdoor Exercise

When AQI is high, exercise indoors or during times when pollution levels are lower.

Report Pollution

Use government apps to report pollution violations from industries or vehicles.
